ABOUT ME

I always dreamed of becoming a Computer Science or Computer Engineering student. But unfortunately, because of some circumstances, I landed a program different from what I desired. A program for future teachers majoring in Information Communication Technology. Although related, their focus is not deep in programming.

In my third year in college, I had a Web Development class, and this course changed my goal for the future. At first, my goal was broad and naive. I never thought that programming was vast and divided into different paths, but everything changed after I took that class.

Taking the class makes me very excited like a kid learning for the first time. I was afraid at first because I didn't have any background in programming, and I knew nothing about it. But all that anxiety faded away after writing my first code. Being able to create and design something out of code makes me very excited and pumped up. It also makes my creativity go wild.

I took my internship or what we call field study and practice teaching in my fourth year. I became a pre-service teacher for 8 months teaching Grade 10-ICT students near Binondo Manila. And now graduating, I still want to pursue web development and be in an environment filled with enthusiasts in programming.
